<https://www.kaggle.com/competitions/playground-series-s5e5>

Solution Writeup · 8th place · Jun 1, 2025

Despite my last submission being 16 days old, I was surprised to secure
8th place in the final standings. My solution utilized a weighted
ensemble optimized via a Hill Climbing algorithm. The final ensemble
combined five distinct test predictions: three TensorFlow models, one
CatBoost model, and one XGBoost model.

My primary objective in this competition was to deepen my understanding
of TensorFlow through hands-on practice. By focusing on tensor
manipulations and library-specific architectures, I was able to build
specialized models that contributed significantly to the diversity and
overall performance of the final ensemble."

*Notebooks Ensemble of Predictions Summary*

<https://www.kaggle.com/code/jiaoyouzhang/calorie-only-xgboost>. Thanks
to @jiaoyouzhang <https://www.kaggle.com/jiaoyouzhang>

<https://www.kaggle.com/chrisk321> .You can find it here. <https://

The rest below are all tensorflow from my private notebooks:
Notebook #F_1025, F_1021 and F_1024

I did not bother optimizing or doing some unique stuff on the public
notebooks. I just mixed this up with my Tensorflow models and call it a
day.
