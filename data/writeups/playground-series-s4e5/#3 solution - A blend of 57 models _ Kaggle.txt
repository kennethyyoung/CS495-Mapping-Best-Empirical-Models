  #3 solution - A blend of 57 models

Regression with a Flood Prediction Dataset

<https://www.kaggle.com/competitions/playground-series-s4e5>

Solution Writeup · 3rd place · May 31, 2024

My solution was a Lasso blend of 57 models, some of which were AutoGluon
ensembles. See model coefficients below.

After about 5 models there were really no changes except on the 5th or
6th decimal place. The two best individual method models (LigtGBM and
CatBoost) were at private LB score 0.86890, while the best AutoML
ensemble was created by AutoGluon (private score 0.86893). The master
ensemble is barely better at private LB score 0.86902. But hey, we are
competing here.

Good correlation between CV scores and public/private LBs.
